    Tutorial

    Welcome to "Rent Race: Surviving Landlord Ronald". Here's how to play:

    • Objective: Survive 12 months by paying rent on time while managing your daily expenses and income.
    • Starting Balance: You start with $100.
    • Daily Activities:
      • Work Job: Earn $112 per day. Click "Work Job" to work and advance the day.
      • Invest in Crypto: Choose an amount to invest. You can multiply your investment or lose it.
      • Pay Rent: Pay rent on or before the 5th of each month to avoid late fees and eviction.
      • Take Loan: Choose a loan amount and duration. Loans have a 10% interest rate per month.
    • Monthly Summary: A summary is provided at the end of each month.
    • Game Over: If you can't pay rent by the 5th, you get evicted and it's game over.
    • Win Condition: Successfully pay rent for all 12 months to win.
